Former President Trump Second Assassination Attempt

By chris on 2024-09-17

On Sunday, September 15, 2024, while Trump was playing a rather public round of golf at his Trump International Golf Club, Ryan Wesley Routh, 58, was in the bushes near the 5th hole. When agents approached the hole to secure it, one agent saw the barrel of a gun (identified as an AK-47) sticking out of the bushes. The agent fired 4 - 6 shots at the suspect. Routh fled and a chase ensued. He was captured a short while later. Routh was 400 - 500 yards away from former President Trump, so he was within range, but he did not have a clear shot, and he never fired a shot, so this would be more of an potential assassination attempt. Some are blaming this on a staffing shortage at the Secret Service. They are down about 400 agents from what they are budgeted for, and they didn't secure the entire course like they might have when Trump was president.
