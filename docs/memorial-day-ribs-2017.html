Memorial Day Ribs 2017

By chris on 2017-05-30

I cooked ribs again this year, but the level of enthusiasm was really low: I didn't cook them until Memorial Day Monday, and no one suggested/volunteered sides. I bought 2 packs seasoned - "Kansas Style" seasoning - in the bag ribs. I cooked them in the oven covered for 2 hours, then based with sauce and uncovered for 1 hour, then re-based and covered with sauce for 1 hour. They came out great. So I think I found the secret. I made store bought beans and frozen corn for sides.
