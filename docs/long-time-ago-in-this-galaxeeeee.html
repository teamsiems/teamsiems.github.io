Long time ago in this galaxeeeee ...

By chris on 2005-05-25

aka ROTS courtesy amazon.com

If you've been underground for the past 28 years here's some shocking news - they made a prequil to Star Wars. No seriously! It's the biggest thing since, well since Star Wars: A New Hope came out May 25, 1977. It's called *Revenge of the Sith* and it grossed about \$150 million - that's right Million - dollars its opening weekend. This caps the epic which is Star Wars - 6 movies/episodes in all (that's 12+ hours for you mini-series fans).

It's a good flick - hell all six movies are *good* - but it was all the more special for those of us that remember the begining of the saga; a long time ago in a galaxy far far away.
