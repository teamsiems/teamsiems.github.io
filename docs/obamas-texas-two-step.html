Obama's Texas Two-Step

By chris on 2008-02-26

A man, a plan, Texas. He's reaching out in a big way. read more | digg story Now for the other Two-Step story ... Yesterday, February 25, I got a flyer in the mail. It was Obama's "Texas Two-Step" campaign. The gist of the message was that we should attend the voting location twice! The first time, it explained, 2/3 of delegates are chosen. The second time - the night of the March 4 primary - you go to the polling location and sign-in thus choosing the remaining 1/3 of the delgates. Huh? Texas doesn't have caucuses. We vote in the Primary then we vote again in the General Election. End of story. UPDATE: Let me rephrase. Texas (a majority Republican state), does not have a Republican caucus. Texas Democrats get to caucus in tight races when there's more than one canidate left at the time of the Texas Primary.
