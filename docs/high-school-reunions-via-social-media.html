High School Reunions Via Social Media

By chris on 2010-01-27

If it wasn't for the Internet and the explosion of social media I probably would not know about my high school reunion. We started finding each other through email before the last reunion in 2000, but facebook, myspace and twitter make finding and communicating a lot easier and cheaper than some places (ClassMates.com). Although the other sites may have served a purpose in the past, social media sites are king today. They're free and more and more people are using them for way more than class reunions.
