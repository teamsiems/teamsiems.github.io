The 2026 Primary Election in Texas

By chris on 2026-02-03

The primary election in Texas will be held on March 3 this year with early voting February 17-27, and the general election will be held November 3, with early voting October 19-30. If no candidate receives a majority of the vote in the primary, the top two vote-getters will compete in a runoff on May 26, with early voting May 18-22. The Texas Tribune has published a useful March primary ballot article.
