Active Gulf Storms

By chris on 2008-08-27

In aviation there's a saying that planes in a line on radar are called a "string of pearls." Well, the Gulf and Atlantic have a string of storm pearls. It has been a relatively quiet season. Tropical Storm Fay came through Florida a couple weeks ago and flooded a lot of places, but I don't think it killed anyone. Fay and T.S. Edouard did help knock loose the high pressure sitting over Texas and we subsequently got some rain after that.
