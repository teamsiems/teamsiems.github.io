Prediction for 2021 Hurricane Season

By chris on 2021-04-09

Broken record here...

This year, Philip Klotzbach, hurricane researcher at Colorado State University, tweeted we should expect an above-average season because of the lack of #ElNino. CSU predicts 17 named storms (1 more than last year), 8 hurricanes, and 4 major (Cat 3+) hurricanes.
