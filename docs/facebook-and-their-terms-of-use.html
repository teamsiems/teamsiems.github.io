Facebook and their Terms of Use

By chris on 2009-02-18

> It appears in the wake of [global attention and outcry](http://consumerist.com/5155538/facebook-privacy-fallout-goes-nuclear), Facebook has, as of at least 12:27 am, reverted back to the previous Terms of Service. Phew, now we can all go back to sending each other digital cupcakes without Big Brother watching us. This is a temporary move until Facebook can draft a new Terms of Service that addresses the users' concerns. -- (The now old) [Facebook Terms of Use](http://www.facebook.com/terms.php)
