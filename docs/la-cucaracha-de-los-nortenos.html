La Cucaracha de Los Nortenos

By chris on 2009-03-25

Los Nortenos Mexican Cafe was shut down Tuesday after health inspectors discovered live roaches.

Inspectors were inspecting the restaurant because of a roach complaint. Health Department officials said they found several of the insects in the drawers and cabinets alongside to-go packets.

Pest control was contacted and health inspectors will do a follow-up inspection Wednesday.

The restaurant will have to pay a \$500 fine.

They reopened the next day, but I'm not going back.
