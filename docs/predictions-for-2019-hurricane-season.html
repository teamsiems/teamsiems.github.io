Predictions for 2019 Hurricane Season

By chris on 2019-04-04

Philip Klotzbach, hurricane researcher at Colorado State University, tweeted that this year's forecast calls for a slightly below average season with at least 13 named storms, five hurricanes and two major hurricanes. The reason for the weaker season is the continuation of a weak El Niño. So, here's to 2019. The season begins June 1.
