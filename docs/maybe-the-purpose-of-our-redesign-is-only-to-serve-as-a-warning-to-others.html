Get input; focus groups, meetings with stakeholders. Plan your content. Number 1 reason it fails is because of content. Make information architecture(?). Make wireframes. Keep track of every piece; inventory the content. By doing all this stuff it defused negative reactions for future designs. Frameworks = no budget friendly. Write a bunch of extra (custom) stuff. Take Away: - get buy-in and high-level ownership - make sure you have the right people on your team - clearly define the project and its scope - do the research - get input and feedback - have a content strategy and plan
