Mental Haze

By chris on 2005-04-12

One day I was driving home from work and saw this view of clouds. I don't know about you, but after a hard day's work I feel like I'm in a haze - mentally exhausted - and so I took this picture to make a visual footnote of how it feels.

I suppose this could represent several things: mood, texture, timing, etc. But that day it was my mental haze.
