Some Recycleable Plastic Bottles Are Not Safe

By chris on 2008-06-10

From Plastic Bottles That Are Not Safe - Toxins in Water Bottles, Baby Bottles at thedailygreen.com

Check the recycle numbers on your plastic containers. If it is a 3, 6, or 7 it can leach bisphenol-A (BPA) into the food/liquids you put in it.
