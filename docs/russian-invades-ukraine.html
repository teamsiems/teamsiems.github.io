Russia Invades Ukraine

By chris on 2022-03-06

\

Vlad the (Impudent) Impaler\

Have you seen this guy? Of course you have. It's ol' Vlad. The Putinator. The Russian President for Life. Well, about 10 days ago he went off his meds and attacked - excuse me, launched a 'special military operation' - in the sovereign nation of Ukraine with soldiers, tanks, bombs, missiles, etc. In the past 10 days over 1 million people have crossed the border to neighboring Poland, Romania, Moldova and Hungary. Estimates range from 136 to 2,000 Ukrainians have been killed so far. Some of the Ukrainians fought back with what else - the quintessential weapon of choice - Molotov cocktails made by an Ukrainian brewery.
