Flight Simulator X

By chris on 2006-10-26

Just keep saying to yourself, "It's only a virtual world. It's only a virtual world.." Flight Simulator 10 (FSX) was released on October 17, 2006, and like its predecessors - it's as real as it gets. Microsoft went back to a two version packaging: standard and deluxe. Of course the deluxe has more stuff. Of course everyone's gonna want to get the deluxe - duh! At last glance (about 60 seconds ago) the deluxe retails for \$69.99. The best deal I saw was tigerdirect.com at \$59.99. Although MS didn't say it won't run great on XP systems, they do recommend a Vista PC - or one that is Vista compliant. (Vista hasn't even officially been release yet!) For this reason I think it wise to hold off on FSX until I have a new screaming Vista to enjoy my VFR vistas.
