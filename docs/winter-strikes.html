Winter Strikes

By chris on 2005-12-08

Yes that is ice. Winter hit last night in central Texas, and tempertures fell into the teens in Dallas and upper 20's south of Waco. It was cold enough and icy enough that work closed today. We usually get one "ice day" per year, but this is a little early in the season for it; winter doesn't start for another 12 days.
