Texas Reds Steak and Grape Festival 2010

By chris on 2010-05-18

It seems like each year about this time my mouth starts watering for steak and wine. Actually, I seem to yearn for them earlier and earlier each year. Last years' Steak & Grape was good but very crowded. This year, the Texas Steak and Grape Festival celebrates it 4th anniversary, June 18-19. They also have a spiffy new (Wordpress) Web site, and I think the City of Bryan is sponsoring it; they're hosting the site. I may be out of town that week so I don't know if I'll make it to the festival. I'll just have to have some steak and grapes in honor of the festival somewhere else.
