[su_youtube url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UEkACZCh5uE" responsive="yes"] **"Three Bullet Points"** 1] The conference is going well, but the elevators are slow as hell. I was trying to rush to get back before 5:30 when the Hannon Hill beer tour bus left. I didn't make it. 3] Maybe it was for the best. The rain is supposed to continue off and on until Wednesday, and I don't have an umbrella. #vedo14 or #vlogtober
