I've been tracking [COVID-19 news](https://teamsiems.com/2020/03/coronavirus-disease-2019-covid-19/) all year. America was the worst at spreading the disease - over 20 million cases and 340,000 deaths - but we made a vaccine (2 in fact) in record time. The first vaccine was administered just 9 months after the WHO declared COVID-19 was a global pandemic (March 11, 2020). Now America is the slowest country to administer the vaccines. Something like 1.3 million doses have been delivered, but only a fraction of that have been administered; inoculation requires 2 doses.
