Title: Worst Week of Winter Weather. Ever.
Date: 2021-02-17 12:00
Author: chris
Category: Environment
Tags: #5daysofwinter, snow, winter
Slug: worst-week-of-winter-weather-ever
Status: published

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

I've lived in this area for 25 years, and I've watched the [weather change over that time](https://teamsiems.com/tag/weather/), but I have never seen weather like this, last this long. For a solid 7 days temperatures have not gotten above 40 F. (Thursday 2/11 high was 37 F.) We had [rolling power outages](https://twitter.com/teamsiems/status/1361520088638193665) on Sunday (2/14) and Monday. We got every kind of precipitation - rain, sleet, freezing rain, and snow. The lowest temperature was 5 F Tuesday morning.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

Fortunately for us, we were prepared. We saw the signs and listened to the forecast, and we ordered grocery pickup on Saturday (2/13) before the food ran out and the bad weather hit. By Monday, HEB was the only place open.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

I camped-out in my living room Sunday - Thursday, tending dripping faucets, watching tree limbs sag, turning on heaters when the power came back on. Texas A&M cancelled classes and work for non-essential employees Monday - Thursday. "Warm up shelters" (that's new) were setup Tuesday - Thursday at local arenas.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
