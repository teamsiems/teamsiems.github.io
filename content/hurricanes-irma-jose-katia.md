While people from Corpus Christi to Houston are still dealing with the [aftermath of Hurricane Harvey](http://teamsiems.com/2017/08/hurricane-harvey/), two more storms showed up in the Atlantic.
[Hurricane Irma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hurricane\_Irma) formed in the mid-Atlantic and strengthened to Category 5 by September 5, 2017; max winds 160 KT (185 mph). Now, it's on the doorstep of the [Leeward Islands](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Leeward+Islands/@15.0942947,-64.5530482,7z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x8c14b8cba6b3d1a3:0xa3be1b25e73d5504!8m2!3d15.1053493!4d-62.3090803). Tropical Storm Jose formed yesterday in the mid-Atlantic.
Here are the advisories from the [National Hurricane Center](http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/)

\*\*\[UPDATE 09-07-2017\]\*\* Tropical Storm Jose strengthened to Hurricane Jose overnight, and Tropical Storm Katia strengthened to Hurricane Katia overnight. \*\*\[UPDATE 09-08-2017\]\*\* In the past 12 hours, Hurricane Irma weakened to Category 4, Hurricane Jose strengthened to Category 4, and Hurricane Katia strengthened to Category 2. Hurricane Irma is located between [Cuba](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Cuba/@21.4551211,-84.0570651,6z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m13!1m7!3m6!1s0x88d69a3bb2480f3d:0x133eb4836ac779e5!2sThe+Bahamas!3b1!8m2!3d25.03428!4d-77.39628!3m4!1s0x88cd49070f7a4cb5:0x798cf7529110a41a!8m2!3d21.6114724!4d-78.9642334) and the [Bahamas](https://www.google.com/maps/place/The+Bahamas/@24.3681124,-80.4600836,6z/data=!4m13!1m7!3m6!1s0x88d69a3bb2480f3d:0x133eb4836ac779e5!2sThe+Bahamas!3b1!8m2!3d25.03428!4d-77.39628!3m4!1s0x88d69a3bb2480f3d:0x133eb4836ac779e5!8m2!3d25.03428!4d-77.39628), and is expected to make landfall in south Florida on Sunday morning. Hurricane Jose is on a path to skim the northern [Leeward Islands](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Leeward+Islands/@15.0942947,-64.5530482,7z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x8c14b8cba6b3d1a3:0xa3be1b25e73d5504!8m2!3d15.1053493!4d-62.3090803) and continue a northerly course towards Bermuda. Hurricane Katia is expected to make landfall north of [Veracruz](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Heroica+Veracruz,+Ver.,+Mexico/@19.1787635,-96.2113356,13z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x85c3414245ca78c5:0x18a4d642e936019b!8m2!3d19.173773!4d-96.1342241), Mexico, late Friday night.
