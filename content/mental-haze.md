[![](http://teamsiems.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/Picture-001-150x150.jpg "clouded-judgement")](http://teamsiems.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/Picture-001.jpg)One day I was driving home from work and saw this view of clouds. I don't know about you, but after a hard day's work I feel like I'm in a haze - mentally exhausted - and so I took this picture to make a visual footnote of how it feels.

I suppose this could represent several things: mood, texture, timing, etc. But that day it was my mental haze.
