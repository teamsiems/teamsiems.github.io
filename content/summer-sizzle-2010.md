It's August in Texas and that means it's time for Summer Sizzle - the bi-annual event [Fort Worth Virtual ARTCC hosts](http://www.zfwartcc.com/pilots/events.php?id=213&m=8) live in Dallas.

[Unlike other events](http://teamsiems.com/2010/05/game-break-is-a-bust/), once we commit we have to be there because hundreds of people around the world are expecting us to show up and provide good air traffic control.

See [my photo set on Flickr](http://www.flickr.com/photos/teamsiems/sets/72157624656068023/) for ZFW Summer Sizzle 2010. This year we had 8 guys show up, and my computer didn't want to start up so I didn't control - so 7 active controllers.
