It may have been a dog's life, but Moose, the Jack Russell terrier of [Frasier](http://www.eonline.com/Facts/Tv/0,1588,14,00.html) fame, wasn't one to complain.
[Kelsey Grammer](http://www.eonline.com/Facts/People/Bio/0,128,25099,00.html)'s canine sidekick, known as Eddie on the long-running show, died Thursday at the ripe old age of 16 and a half, his trainer, Mathilde Halberg told People magazine.
