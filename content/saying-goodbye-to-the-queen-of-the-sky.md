After 54 years of production, Boeing rolled out its [last 747 this week](https://www.npr.org/2022/12/08/1141578966/boeing-747-last-jet). The 747 was so big they called it "jumbo jet", yet so elegant they called it the [queen of the sky](https://www.npr.org/2017/11/07/562486236/united-says-goodbye-to-its-last-boeing-747-with-a-final-flight).
