Status: published

With ever [mounting deaths](https://teamsiems.com/2020/03/coronavirus-disease-2019-covid-19/), scientists have been under pressure to develop a COVID-19 vaccine.
[Moderna](https://www.modernatx.com/), Inc., reported that they have developed a COVID-19 vaccine that is safe. Phase I testing (45 subjects) appears to elicit the kind of immune response capable of preventing the disease. There were no no serious side-effects after the first trial. Source: [NPR](https://www.npr.org/sections/coronavirus-live-updates/2020/05/18/857997341/new-coronavirus-vaccine-candidate-shows-promise-in-early-limited-trial)
Phase II can start right away, and the company hopes to start phase III in early July.
