Bush, Bill Clinton, George H.W. Bush and Jimmy Carter will attend the event at Reed Arena at Texas A&M University. Source [CNN.com](http://www.cnn.com/2017/10/21/politics/hurricane-relief-concert-five-former-presidents/index.html)

They'll be in town Saturday to bolster donations and support for hurricane relief. Bush 41 wanted to do something bigger than a anniversary party for his library - it was his idea to have a concert and start a fund raising campaign - [One America Appeal](https://www.oneamericaappeal.org/).

The weather isn't cooperating. There is a 30% chance of rain during the day increasing to 90% chance at night.
