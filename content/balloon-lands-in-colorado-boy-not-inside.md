By Keith Coffman

> DENVER (Reuters) - A 6-year-old boy, \[Falcon Heene,\] said to have climbed into a homemade helium balloon that took off and flew across Colorado on Thursday, was not inside when the contraption landed and authorities launched a search for him.

Source: [Reuters](http://www.reuters.com/article/domesticNews/idUSTRE59E5H720091015).

The response on twitter was even faster. The hastag [\#balloonboy](http://twitter.com/search?q=%23balloonboy) shot to top trend in a matter of hours. A [website](http://whereisballoonboy.com/) was created, and of course there was a t-shirt, "[Go Falcon, Go](http://www.zazzle.com/go_falcon_go_tshirt-235529491037592548)."

The back story is that the 6-year old was a member of the family that was on the "Wife Swap" show. You can see Falcon in this [youtube video](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EBWJXXgaYBo).
