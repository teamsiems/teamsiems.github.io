Apparently I'm not the only one to think of [the idea](http://www.truthorfiction.com/rumors/f/fuelprotest.htm). If each person stockpiled gas - especially at a lower price - then, at some predetermined date, we all stop buying gas.

I realize the size of your stockpile would be big - proportional to size of your family and your "pain" tolerance to stop driving your gas-sucking SUV - but think of the possibilities.
