Two great movies every one should see. Although [Easy Rider](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easy_Rider) (1969) received more acclaim, [Vanishing Point](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vanishing_Point_(1971_film)) (1971) is the film people can't seem to forget. They are driving films going in opposite directions: Easy Rider is Los Angeles going to New Orleans (hippies meet voodoo), and Vanishing Point is Denver going to San Francisco (mile-high meet hippies). Easy Rider is chock-full of stars. Vanishing Point has relatively unknown actors.

Another one I just thought of is [Network](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Network_(film)) (1976). In 2000, the film was selected for [preservation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Film_Preservation_Board "National Film Preservation Board") in the United States [National Film Registry](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Film_Registry "National Film Registry") by the [Library of Congress](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Library_of_Congress "Library of Congress") as being "culturally, historically, or aesthetically significant".
