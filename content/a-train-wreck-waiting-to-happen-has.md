Speaking of crowded bullet trains, a Japanese train [crashed](http://www.wsws.org/articles/2005/may2005/jap-m11.shtml) on 25 April killing 107 and injuring 400+. They chaulked it up to driver stress. Wait, bullet train drivers in Japan get stressed? No shit?! In a country [obsessed with punctuality](http://www.japantimes.co.jp/cgi-bin/getarticle.pl5?nn20050526f2.htm) I guess I could kinda see why. I guess if you had to deliver millions of people to and from work at insain speeds you'd get a little stressed too. But wait, 'ordinary' people do that in L.A. on a daily basis, and when they wig out it's called '[road rage](http://www.dailynews.com/Stories/0,1413,200~20954~2866613,00.html).'
