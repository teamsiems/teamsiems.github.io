We’re starting this post early this year with the <a href="https://x.com/philklotzbach/status/1907795220374188228" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">annual tweet</a> from Philip that calls for a above-normal year with 17 named storms, 9 hurricanes, and 4 major hurricanes. He adds that a “relatively warm Atlantic and likely absence of <a href="https://x.com/hashtag/ElNino?src=hashtag_click" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">#ElNino</a> are the primary reasons.”
