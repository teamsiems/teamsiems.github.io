> After turning back last-minute attempts to let city voters opt out, the Texas House gave final approval Monday to legislation allowing gun owners with concealed weapons licenses to carry their side arms openly.
>
> House Bill 910, by Rep. <a href="http://www.texastribune.org/directory/larry-phillips/" data-tooltip="/directory/larry-phillips/quicklook/">Larry Phillips</a>, R- Sherman, passed on third reading 101-42. Similar legislation has already passed the Senate, and Gov. <a href="http://www.texastribune.org/directory/greg-abbott/" data-tooltip="/directory/greg-abbott/quicklook/">Greg Abbott</a> has promised to sign open carry legislation. Source: [Texas Tribune](https://www.texastribune.org/2015/04/20/open-carry-headed-governors-desk/)

Back in January, [I was watching SB11 and HB937](http://teamsiems.com/2015/01/texas-bills-related-to-concealed-carry/), relating to concealed carry on college campuses, but thanks to lobbying by [Open Carry Texas](http://www.opencarrytexas.org/) and the NRA, SB11 and HB910 seem to be doing well. Both bills would relax the concealment requirement of the Concealed Handgun License (CHL) holders.
