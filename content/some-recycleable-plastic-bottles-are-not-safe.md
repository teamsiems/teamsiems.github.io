From [Plastic Bottles That Are Not Safe - Toxins in Water Bottles, Baby Bottles](http://www.thedailygreen.com/green-homes/eco-friendly/plastic-bottles-toxins-water-bottles-460410) at [thedailygreen.com](http://www.thedailygreen.com/)

Check the recycle numbers on your plastic containers. If it is a 3, 6, or 7 it can leach bisphenol-A (BPA) into the food/liquids you put in it.
