It's been a little over ten years since Andrea Yates was found insane and [I wrote about the verdict](http://teamsiems.com/2006/07/jury-finds-yates-insane-not-guilty/).

Fifteen years after drowning her five children in the bathtub of her suburban Houston home, Andrea Yates lives a reclusive life in a Texas mental health facility and frequently watches videos of her children laughing and playing, sources close to Yates tell PEOPLE. Source: [People.com](http://www.people.com/article/andrea-yates-drown-five-children-mental-health-facility-texas)

It continues to be a sad end to a sad case.
