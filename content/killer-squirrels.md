I've witnessed my first flesh-eating squirrel. He sat for over an hour munching away on a dead bird outside my workplace.

Come to find out [killer squirrels](http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=killer+squirrels&btnG=Google+Search) are a common lot. Several sites are dedicated to squirrel attacks - usually cyclists or motor-bikers. Someone even came up with the phrase of [The World Organisation Of Killer Squirrels](http://www.20six.co.uk/DJJaines) (TWOOKS). A catchy title, but it's just another blog and has little to do with squirrels.

Many folks like to take [pictures](http://images.google.com/images?q=killer+squirrels&hl=en&lr=&sa=G) of squirrels and call them "killer squirrels" but they aren't really.

If you want to find some really nutty people check the [Groups](http://groups-beta.google.com/groups?hl=en&lr=&q=killer+squirrels&qt_s=Search+Groups) on this topic - oh boy. Some write about killer squirrels from outer space. [Teams](http://www.killersquirrels.com/html/) of game players use the name as a badge of honor.

Humans definitely dedicated a lot of time to killer squirrels.
