The General Election in Texas is November 4, 2014. Early voting runs October 20-31, 2014. From the [Primary Election](http://teamsiems.com/2014/02/the-2014-primary-election-in-texas/) in March to the General Election in November, the candidates have been chosen. See [who/what is on the ballot](http://votetexas.gov/voting/what) or [table of candidates](http://www.sos.state.tx.us/elections/candidates/general/2014-gen-sbs.shtml).

There are 5 possible party affiliations: R-Republican, D-Democrat, L-Libertarian, G-Green, and I-Independent.

## Amendments to the Texas Constitution

*Proposition 1:*

Allocate to Fund 6 (The State Highway Fund) one-half of the general revenue derived from oil and gas production taxes that currently is transferred to the Economic Stabilization Fund (Rainy Day Fund).
