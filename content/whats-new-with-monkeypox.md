We can now add [monkeypox](https://www.cdc.gov/poxvirus/monkeypox/symptoms.html) to the list of highly contagious, virial diseases that are trying to kill us all.
> \*\*[WHO](https://www.who.int/europe/news/item/23-07-2022-who-director-general-declares-the-ongoing-monkeypox-outbreak-a-public-health-event-of-international-concern) declares monkeypox a public health emergency of international concern\*\*
> Currently, the US is reporting over 2,800 probable or confirmed monkeypox cases in 44 states, DC and Puerto Rico, according to data from the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Globally, there are over 16,500 cases reported in 74 countries.
>
> Source: [CNN.com](https://www.cnn.com/2022/07/23/health/monkeypox-who-intl/index.html) taken July 22, 2022
