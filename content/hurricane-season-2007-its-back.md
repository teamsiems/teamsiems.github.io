Tropical Storm Erin, now a tropical depression, is the first to sweep into the Lonestar state. She arrived 5:00 AM CDT this morning and by 7:00 AM she was downgraded.

[<img src="http://teamsiems.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/Hurricane-Dean-20070816-7821811-150x120.jpg" title="Hurricane-Dean-20070816" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1727" width="150" height="120" />](http://teamsiems.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/Hurricane-Dean-20070816-7821811.jpg)Off on the horizon - in the Atlantic - is her big brother, Hurricane Dean. Dean must have been promoted to hurricane within the past few hours (maybe 5:00 AM CDT). We should expect him to strengthen and head straight for the Caribbean (Jamaica) and points west.
