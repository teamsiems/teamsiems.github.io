## German Festivals in Texas

- [Tomball German Heritage Festival](http://www.tomballgermanfest.org/about-us/) \| Tomball, TX \| March 22-24, 2013
- [Germanfest](http://www.germanfest.net/home/) \| Muenster, TX \| April 26-28, 2013
- [Maifest](http://www.maifest.org/play/) \| Brenham, TX \| May 3-5, 2013
- [Oktoberfest](http://www.oktoberfestinfbg.com/) \| Fredericksburg, TX \| October 4-6, 2013
- [Wurstfest](http://www.wurstfest.com/) \| New Braunfels, TX \| November 1-10, 2013
