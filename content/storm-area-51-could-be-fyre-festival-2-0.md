The "[Storm Area 51](https://teamsiems.com/2019/07/storm-area-51/)" organizer, Matty Roberts, is comparing Alienstock - music festival he organized as an alternative to Storm Area 51 - to the [Fyre Festival](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fyre_Festival) - a name that has become synonymous with "epic failure."

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->

> With just over a week to go until the event, Roberts and the host town’s website are both comparing Alienstock to the Fyre Festival, which was supposed to be held in April and May of 2017 in the Bahamas but became synonymous with “epic failure” and led to a fraud conviction. Roberts has pulled his name and support from the three-day gathering in Rachel, Nev., but the owner of a motel in the town who had signed up as a partner plans to go ahead.
>
> <cite>Source: [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2019/09/11/storm-area-creator-pulls-out-his-own-event-calling-it-fyre-festival/)</cite>

<!-- /wp:quote -->
