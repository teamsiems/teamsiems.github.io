If you've been underground for the past 28 years here's some shocking news - they made a prequil to [Star Wars](http://www.starwars.com/). No seriously! It's the biggest thing since, well since Star Wars: A New Hope came out [May 25, 1977](http://www.fromscripttodvd.com/star_wars_a_day_long_remembered.htm). It's called *Revenge of the Sith* and it grossed about \$150 million - that's right Million - dollars its opening weekend. This caps the epic which is Star Wars - 6 movies/episodes in all (that's 12+ hours for you [mini-series fans](http://www.museum.tv/archives/etv/M/htmlM/miniseries/miniseries.htm)).
