[Three weeks ago](http://teamsiems.com/2011/01/daily-bible/) I wrote about using a little technology to help me keep on track with reading the Bible in a year. Well, I went one step further and made a mashup of a PHP calendar with a daily verse list (it was XML), linked each verse to its mobile version of the NIV Bible, and voilà - [a calendar with links to Bible verses](http://m.teamsiems.com/calendar.php).
