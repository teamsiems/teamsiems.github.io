Actually, I seem to yearn for them earlier and earlier each year. [Last years' Steak & Grape](http://teamsiems.com/2009/05/texas-reds-steak-and-grape-festival/) was good but very crowded.

This year, the [Texas Steak and Grape Festival](http://www.bryantx.gov/texasredsfestival/) celebrates it 4th anniversary, June 18-19. They also have a spiffy new (Wordpress) Web site, and I think the City of Bryan is sponsoring it; they're hosting the site.

I may be out of town that week so I don't know if I'll make it to the festival. I'll just have to have some steak and grapes in honor of the festival somewhere else.
