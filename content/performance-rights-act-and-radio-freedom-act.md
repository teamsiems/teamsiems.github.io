I heard about this today. From the http://www.noperformancetax.org/ website:
> There are currently two bills pending in Congress that would levy a performance tax on local radio – [H.R.848](http://www.noperformancetax.org/en/~/media/PDF/CONYER\_bill.ashx), sponsored by Rep. John Conyers (MI-14) and [S.379](http://www.noperformancetax.org/en/~/media/PDF/Leahy\_S379.ashx), sponsored by Sen. Patrick Leahy (VT). Your members of Congress need to hear that you strongly oppose these bills.
>
> Additionally, anti-performance tax resolutions have been introduced in the House and Senate in support of local radio. In the Senate, Sens. Blanche Lincoln (AR) and John Barrasso (WY) introduced [S. Con. Res. 14](http://www.noperformancetax.org/en/~/media/PDF/S%20Con%20Res%2014.ashx), and in the House, Reps. Gene Green (TX-29) and Mike Conaway (TX-11) introduced [H. Con. Res. 49](http://www.noperformancetax.org/en/~/media/PDF/H%20Con%20res%20%2049.ashx). Both are known as the Local Radio Freedom Act. Many members of Congress already support local radio and resolutions against the performance tax. Others still need to hear your voice.
>
> [Take action now](http://www.noperformancetax.org/en/Take%20Action)!
