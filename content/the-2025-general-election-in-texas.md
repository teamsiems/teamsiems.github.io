Elections will take place Tuesday, November 4, 2025. Early voting is October 20-31. This year there are [17 Texas Constitutional amendments](https://www.sos.state.tx.us/elections/forms/november-2025-ballot-language-17.pdf) voters will vote on.

## Voting deadlines

- Voter registration deadline: October 6, 2025.
- Early voting period: October 20, 2025, through October 31, 2025.
- Absentee ballot request deadline: October 24, 2025.
- Election Day: Tuesday, November 4, 2025.
