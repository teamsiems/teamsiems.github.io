<!-- wp:paragraph -->

Elections will take place Tuesday, November 4, 2025. Early voting is October 20-31. This year there are <a href="https://www.sos.state.tx.us/elections/forms/november-2025-ballot-language-17.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">17 Texas Constitutional amendments</a> voters will vote on.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->

## Voting deadlines

<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->

<ul class="wp-block-list">

<!-- wp:list-item -->

</p>

<li>

Voter registration deadline: October 6, 2025.

</li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li>

Early voting period: October 20, 2025, through October 31, 2025.

</li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li>

Absentee ballot request deadline: October 24, 2025.

</li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li>

Election Day: Tuesday, November 4, 2025.

</li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->

</ul>

<!-- /wp:list -->
