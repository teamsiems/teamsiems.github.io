Status: published

The primary election in Texas will be held on March 3 [this year](https://www.sos.state.tx.us/elections/voter/important-election-dates.shtml) with early voting February 17-27, and the general election will be held November 3, with early voting October 19-30. If no candidate receives a majority of the vote in the primary, the top two vote-getters will compete in a runoff on May 26, with early voting May 18-22. The Texas Tribune has published a useful [March primary ballot](https://apps.texastribune.org/features/2026/texas-march-2026-primary-ballot/) article.
