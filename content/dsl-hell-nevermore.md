What a difference a few years makes, but I finally got to use my Linksys router. Back in April 2006 [I went through DSL hell](http://teamsiems.com/2006/07/event-horizon-or-the-infinite-verizon-or-d-s-hell/) trying to connect my wireless router to a DSL modem. It's January 2009, and when we went back to cable internet, I installed the software, went through the configuration (a couple times) and it worked.

I'm writing this now because it's the calm before the storm. I want to remember a time when things worked. Next week I'm moving to a new house and getting a new cable setup. I will go through DSL hell nevermore!
