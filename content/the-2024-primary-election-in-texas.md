Status: published

The primary election in Texas will be held on March 5 [this year](https://www.sos.state.tx.us/elections/voter/important-election-dates.shtml) with early voting starting February 20, and the general election will be held November 5 with early voting starting October 21. If no candidate receives a majority of the vote in the primary, the top two vote-getters will compete in a runoff on May 28. The Texas Tribune has published a useful [March primary ballot](https://apps.texastribune.org/features/2024/march-2024-primary-ballot/) article.
