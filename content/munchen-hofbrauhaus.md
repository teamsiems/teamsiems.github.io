Munich is known for it's bier (beer) so when in Rome...  
The meal at [Hofbräuhaus](http://www.hofbraeuhaus.de/) was very good - like grandma Siems probably would have made if I had known her: 3 types of wurts (sausages), sauerkraut, kartoffelpüree (mashed potatos) and of course 1 litre of Hofbräu dunkel beir (dark beer).
