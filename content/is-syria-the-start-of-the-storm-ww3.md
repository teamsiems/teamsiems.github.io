Last night, the United States, France and the United Kingdom, launched airstrikes against 3 strategic targets in Syria. The strikes were in reliation against alleged chemical weapons attacks that occurred on April 7, 2018. Russia, a Syrian ally, warned such actions will have consequences. Here is a brief timeline of events that have some wondering if we're on the brink of World War III.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:more -->  
<!--more-->  
<!-- /wp:more -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

**April 7, 2017**

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->

> The 2017 Shayrat missile strike took place on the morning of 7 April 2017, and involved the launch of 59 Tomahawk cruise missiles by the United States from the Mediterranean Sea into Syria, aimed at the Shayrat Airbase controlled by the Syrian government. The strike was executed under responsibility of U.S. President Donald Trump, as a direct response to the Khan Shaykhun chemical attack that occurred on 4 April. Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017_Shayrat_missile_strike)

<!-- /wp:quote -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

**October 5, 2017**  
  
President Trump made (another) seemingly random comment to the press at a White House dinner honoring military leaders: "You guys know what this represents? Maybe it’s the **calm before the storm**." Source: [The New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2017/10/06/us/politics/trump-calls-meeting-with-military-leaders-the-calm-before-the-storm.html)

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

The reporters repeatedly asked him "what storm?" He never answered, but he concludes the photo-op by saying "You'll find out."

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

This remark has conspiracy nuts going bonkers (search for [\#CBTS](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23CBTS)).

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

**April 13, 2018**

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->

> On 14 April 2018, beginning at 04:00 Syrian time (UTC+3), the United States, France, and the United Kingdom carried out a series of military strikes, involving manned aircraft and ship-based missiles, against multiple government sites in Syria. The three acting powers did so in response to the use of chemical weapons against people in Douma in eastern Ghouta on 7 April, which they attributed to the Syrian government. Syria denied involvement in the Douma attacks and called the airstrikes a violation of international law. Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018_bombing_of_Damascus_and_Homs)
