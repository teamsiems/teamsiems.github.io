Today, this [xkcd.com comic](http://www.xkcd.com/739/) created an explosion of traffic (and controversy) on search engines and Wikipedia.
> Malamanteau is a combination of two words [malapropism](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malapropism) and [portmanteau](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portmanteau). These two are real words that have real meanings and the meanings are easily available on Wikipedia. Source: [Malamanteau : Meaning of Malamanteau](http://news.puggal.com/malamanteau-meaning-of-malamanteau-35896/)
Wikipedia quickly deleted the word - citing that it's under review.
The power of the web - or the people on the web - never ceases to amaze me. The lesson here kids, don't believe everything you see on Wikipedia - or anywhere on the web for that matter - always verify or confirm a source before you go quoting it in your blog.
