YouTube.com started with one video, "<a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jNQXAC9IVRw" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Me at the zoo</a>", which was uploaded by jawed on this date in 2005. My, how times have changed in 7 short years.

As of 2012, YouTube.com gets over <a href="http://www.youtube.com/t/press_statistics" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">3 billion video views a day</a>, and over 800 million unique users visit YouTube each month.
