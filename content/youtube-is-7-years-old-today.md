YouTube.com started with one video, "[Me at the zoo](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jNQXAC9IVRw)", which was uploaded by jawed on this date in 2005. My, how times have changed in 7 short years.
As of 2012, YouTube.com gets over [3 billion video views a day](http://www.youtube.com/t/press_statistics), and over 800 million unique users visit YouTube each month.
