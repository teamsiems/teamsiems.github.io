The midterm election is drawing near - one week to go - and the field of candidates has changed since I wrote about them in February. There is a new website done by the Texas Secretary of State, votexas.org, that gives "simplified" information on the [who, what, when, where, and how](http://www.votexas.org/who_votes.html) for this election. In particular it gives a list of candidates.

Aside from the facts, there sure is a lot of mud-slinging ads on TV for this election. I'll be glad when November 2 is past.
