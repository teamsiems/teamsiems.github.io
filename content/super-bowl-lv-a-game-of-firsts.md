Super Bowl LV (February 7, 2021) - Kansas City Chiefs vs Tampa Bay Buccaneers - will be a game of firsts.
> The Buccaneers will be the first team to play a Super Bowl in their home stadium,[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super\_Bowl\_LV#cite\_note-8) and the first [wild card](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wild\_card\_(sports) "Wild card (sports)") team to appear in the Super Bowl since the [Green Bay Packers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green\_Bay\_Packers "Green Bay Packers") in [Super Bowl XLV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super\_Bowl\_XLV "Super Bowl XLV"). In his first season as a player for the Buccaneers, quarterback [Tom Brady](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom\_Brady "Tom Brady") will appear in his record 10th Super Bowl.[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super\_Bowl\_LV#cite\_note-9) The Chiefs will seek to become the first team to repeat as Super Bowl champions since Brady's former team the [New England Patriots](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New\_England\_Patriots "New England Patriots") ([XXXVIII](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super\_Bowl\_XXXVIII "Super Bowl XXXVIII") and [XXXIX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super\_Bowl\_XXXIX "Super Bowl XXXIX")).[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super\_Bowl\_LV#cite\_note-10) Due to [COVID-19](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact\_of\_the\_COVID-19\_pandemic\_on\_sports "Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on sports") protocols limiting stadium capacity to 25,000 fans, this will be the lowest attended Super Bowl.[\[11\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super\_Bowl\_LV#cite\_note-official\_attendance\_expected-11)
