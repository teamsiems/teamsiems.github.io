Super Bowl LV (February 7, 2021) - Kansas City Chiefs vs Tampa Bay Buccaneers - will be a game of firsts.

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->

> The Buccaneers will be the first team to play a Super Bowl in their home stadium,<sup>[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Bowl_LV#cite_note-8)</sup> and the first [wild card](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wild_card_(sports) "Wild card (sports)") team to appear in the Super Bowl since the [Green Bay Packers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green_Bay_Packers "Green Bay Packers") in [Super Bowl XLV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Bowl_XLV "Super Bowl XLV"). In his first season as a player for the Buccaneers, quarterback [Tom Brady](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom_Brady "Tom Brady") will appear in his record 10th Super Bowl.<sup>[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Bowl_LV#cite_note-9)</sup> The Chiefs will seek to become the first team to repeat as Super Bowl champions since Brady's former team the [New England Patriots](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_England_Patriots "New England Patriots") ([XXXVIII](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Bowl_XXXVIII "Super Bowl XXXVIII") and [XXXIX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Bowl_XXXIX "Super Bowl XXXIX")).<sup>[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Bowl_LV#cite_note-10)</sup> Due to [COVID-19](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic_on_sports "Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on sports") protocols limiting stadium capacity to 25,000 fans, this will be the lowest attended Super Bowl.<sup>[\[11\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Bowl_LV#cite_note-official_attendance_expected-11)</sup>
>
> <cite>Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Bowl_LV)</cite>
