`teamsiems.com <http://teamsiems.com/>`__ has always been a sandbox for experimenting with Internet technology and developing web applications. This page lists some of the projects we are working on. The `Portfolio </portfolio/>`__ page highlights some of the successful uses of these and other projects.

| `teamsiems mobile <http://m.teamsiems.com/>`__
| Using mobile frameworks to produce responsive web pages for any device. It will become my home page when iGoogle goes away.

| `Noms For The Win <http://nomsftw.teamsiems.com/>`__
| A food/recipe blog that my wife and I started.

| `Siems2000 <http://siems2000.teamsiems.com/>`__
| One of my first web sites. I made it when Y2K was something to fear.

After much debate, I decided to take down *teamsiems tweets*. It never worked well, and it was consuming a lot of host processes. I still have the Twitter account at @\ `teamsiems <https://twitter.com/teamsiems>`__. I also removed *Adventures in Flying*. It had not been updated in years.
